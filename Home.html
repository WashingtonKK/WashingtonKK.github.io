NAKUJA PROJECT

Nakuja Project is a project whose goal is to develop a liquid fuel that will go past the Karman line (100 Km).
It is comprised mainly of JKUAT undergraduate students taking a variety of courses such as Mechatronics, Telecommunication and Information Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering.

The project is divided into 4 thematic areas, Propulsion, Avionics, Airframes and Flight Control.

I am the leader of the propulsion team, I recently coordinated the team in the development of a Solid Propellant that got the Nakuja-2 rocket to an altitude of 280 metres.
